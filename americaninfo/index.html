Introduction

American Information strives to help Americans just like you to remember our great history! With our archives pulled from U.S. historians, libraries, and even museums, you too, can become the knowledgeable American we all want to be! Be a Patriot of America!

Purpose

Do you usually find yourself forgetting crucial heritage information? Did Benedict Arnold try to betray us to the British, or was he just a double agent? Do you have an upcoming U.S. history test, and the material the professor gave you is not good enough? If you answered yes, no, maybe, or don't care on either of the questions then you have come to the right place!

Story

I was born and raised in Sweden for 16 years before getting the God blessed opportunity to move to the USA. I have as of now lived here for 8 years, but the lack of knowledge the people have about their own history is very sad. As someone who truly love the United States of America, even though I was not born here, my wish is that we all can get the information we need to not forget the past.
