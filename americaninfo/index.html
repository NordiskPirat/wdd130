Information about us

Patriots of America! Do you usually find yourself forgetting crucial heritage information? Did Benedict Arnold try and betray us to the British, or was he just a double agent? Do you have an upcoming U.S. history test, and the material the professor gave you is not good enough? If you answered yes, no, maybe, or don't care on either of the questions then you have come to the right place!

What is our purpose?

Patriots of America strives to help Americans just like you to remember our great history! With our archives pulled from U.S. historians, libraries, and even museums, you too, can become the knowledgeable American we all want to be!
