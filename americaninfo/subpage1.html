The War, a brief summary

The civil war was a time of unrest in the U.S.A. It broke out because the southern states wanted to keep slaves, have more states rights, and other disputes. They seceded from the states, splitting the country into a north and south, Union and Confederacy. Slavery was a constant conflict in politics and a big reason of the war. Lincoln who opposed slavery expansion won the election for presidency. That finalized the ceceding. The war lasted for 4 years and ended with the North as the victors.

Statistics

Union statistics are as followed: The total dead reached a number of 365,000. 110,000 were killed in action, 230,000 died from accidents or diseases, 25,000-30,000 died in Confederate prisons. 282,000 were wounded and 181,193 captured. Total casualties and losses: 828,000+. Confederate statistics are as followed: The total dead reacher a number of 290,000. 94,000 killed in action, 26,000-31,000 died in Union prisons. 137,000 were wounded and 436,658 captured. Total casualties and losses: 864,000+.

Abraham Lincoln

The president at the time was Abraham lincoln. He was born in Kentucky 1809 to "undistinguished parents." He was did not know much as he grew up, but yet he managed to learn how to read, write, and cipher. He worked on his parents farm. He met Mary Todd and married her. They had four kids together, all boys. When he became president he built up the Republican party into a stronger organizaion. When the civil war broke out showed great capacity as President. He gave the Gettysburg address, read the emancipation declaration, and guided the country to the end of the war. After the war was won, Lincoln was killed in a theater after having been reelected as president. It was on April 14th 1865, and he died a few days later.
