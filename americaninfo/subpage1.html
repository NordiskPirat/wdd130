The War

The civil war was a time of unrest in the U.S.A. It broke out because the southern states wanted to keep slaves, so they seceded from the states, splitting the country into a north and south, union and confederacy. Slavery was a constant conflict in politics and the biggest reason of the war. Lincoln who opposed slavery expansion won the election for presidency. That finalised the ceceding. The war lasted for 4 years and ended with the North as the victors.

Statistics

About 10% of the casualties during the war came from military prisons. The union's navy was to block confederate ships and take control of rivers. It had 45 thousand sailors in 1865.

Abraham Lincoln

The president at the time was Abraham lincoln. He gave the Gettysburg address. After the war was won Lincoln was killed in a theater.
